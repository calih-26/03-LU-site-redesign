About Fran

Francine Miller

Francine Miller founded Fran's Flowers in 1995 with a simple mission: to bring joy, beauty, and connection to the Mission District of San Francisco. What started as a small storefront with a passion for flowers has grown into a beloved community institution.

A Vision of Beauty and Service

Fran believed that flowers have the power to transform moments into memories. Whether it's a birthday celebration, a wedding, a gesture of sympathy, or simply brightening someone's day, every arrangement that leaves the shop carries that belief forward.

Community First

From the very beginning, Fran's Flowers has been committed to giving back. Ten percent of every sale supports efforts to fight homelessness across the Bay Area. For over thirty years, this commitment has remained at the heart of everything we do.

Local and Sustainable

Fran has always prioritized sourcing flowers locally whenever possible. By supporting local growers and using sustainable practices, we ensure that our beautiful bouquets are kind to both our community and our planet.

Then and Now

Today, Fran's Flowers continues to thrive under the same values that inspired its founding. Our team of skilled florists works daily to create stunning arrangements that celebrate life's special moments while honoring Fran's vision of spreading kindness one flower at a time.
